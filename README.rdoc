== README

*Start on Forms page to decide which form you'd like to fill out: Schools or Life Events.

*Schools: You can fill out where you went to school, when you started, and when you left. All
forms MUST be filled out to submit.

*Life Events: You can fill out the event title, the date it occurred, and a brief
 description. **NOTE** Your description of your life event may not exceed 140 characters.
 Sorry, them's the rules.
